About Us

Where Stories Find Their Voice

Syreya Recording Theatre is a premier post-production audio studio nestled in the heart of the Tamil film industry. With over a decade of experience crafting sonic identities for cinema, we combine cutting-edge technology with an unwavering passion for storytelling.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses acoustically treated studios, industry-grade consoles, and a team of seasoned audio engineers who understand the language of emotion. From independent productions to major studio features, every project receives the same meticulous attention to detail.

What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end audio post-production under one roof — crafted with precision, delivered with excellence.

Dubbing

Professional voice dubbing in multiple languages with lip-sync precision. Our booths are equipped for actors to deliver natural, expressive performances every take.

Sound Effects (SFX)

Custom-designed sound effects that breathe life into visuals. From subtle ambience to explosive action — our library and bespoke design process covers every scene.

Foley

Immersive, hand-crafted Foley artistry that places audiences right inside the scene. Our dedicated Foley stage handles footsteps, cloth, props, and environment with finesse.

Music Production

Orchestral scores, background music, and original compositions that amplify the emotional arc of your film. Full arrangement, recording, and mixing in-house.

Mixing & Mastering
